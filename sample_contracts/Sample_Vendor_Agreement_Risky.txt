VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of May 1, 2025,

by and between:

Global Retail Corp., a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 3000 Commerce Way, Chicago, IL 60601 ("Company")

and

QuickShip Logistics Ltd., a company registered in the United Kingdom with offices at 10 Canary Wharf, London E14 5AB ("Vendor")

NOTE: This contract contains several intentionally risky and unfavorable clauses for demonstration purposes.

1. SERVICES

The Vendor agrees to provide the following logistics and fulfillment services:
(a) Warehousing and inventory management for up to 500,000 SKUs;
(b) Order fulfillment and shipping across North America and Europe;
(c) Returns processing and reverse logistics;
(d) Real-time inventory tracking and reporting via API integration.

2. TERM AND AUTO-RENEWAL

2.1 This Agreement shall be effective for an initial term of five (5) years from the Effective Date.

2.2 THIS AGREEMENT SHALL AUTOMATICALLY RENEW FOR SUCCESSIVE FIVE (5) YEAR TERMS unless the Company provides written notice of non-renewal at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

[RISK: Extremely long auto-renewal period with very early notice requirement — easy to miss and get locked in for another 5 years]

3. PRICING AND PAYMENT

3.1 Monthly service fees: $250,000 base fee plus per-unit fulfillment charges as per Schedule A.

3.2 Payment terms: Net 15 from invoice date. [RISK: Very aggressive payment terms]

3.3 VENDOR MAY INCREASE FEES BY UP TO 15% ANNUALLY WITH THIRTY (30) DAYS NOTICE, WITHOUT REQUIRING COMPANY'S CONSENT. [RISK: Uncapped unilateral price increases]

3.4 Late Payment Penalty: 3% per month compounding on overdue amounts. [RISK: Excessive late penalty rate]

3.5 Minimum Annual Commitment: Company guarantees minimum annual spend of $3,000,000 regardless of actual usage. [RISK: Take-or-pay obligation even if volumes decrease]

4. EXCLUSIVITY

4.1 DURING THE TERM OF THIS AGREEMENT, COMPANY SHALL USE VENDOR AS ITS SOLE AND EXCLUSIVE LOGISTICS PROVIDER for all fulfillment operations in North America and Europe. Company shall not engage any competing logistics provider without Vendor's prior written consent.

[RISK: Total exclusivity with no carve-outs for specialized shipments or emergency alternatives]

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

5.1 VENDOR'S TOTAL LIABILITY FOR ALL CLAIMS ARISING UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY COMPANY IN THE THREE (3) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

[RISK: Extremely low liability cap — only 3 months of fees for potentially millions in damages]

5.2 IN NO EVENT SHALL VENDOR BE LIABLE FOR: LOST PROFITS, LOST REVENUE, LOSS OF BUSINESS, LOSS OF GOODWILL, LOSS OF DATA, BUSINESS INTERRUPTION, OR ANY INDIRECT, SPECIAL, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES OF ANY KIND.

[RISK: Extremely broad liability exclusion — covers almost all real-world damages]

5.3 Company acknowledges that shipping delays of up to 14 business days shall not constitute a breach of this Agreement.

[RISK: 14-day delay tolerance is excessive for logistics]

6. INDEMNIFICATION (ONE-SIDED)

6.1 Company shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Vendor from any and all claims, damages, losses, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from or relating to:
(a) Company's products or services;
(b) Any injury to persons or property caused by Company's products;
(c) Any third-party claims related to Company's business operations;
(d) Company's failure to comply with applicable laws.

6.2 Vendor shall have no indemnification obligations to Company under this Agreement.

[RISK: Completely one-sided indemnification — Company bears all risk]

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 ALL DATA, ANALYTICS, ALGORITHMS, AND INSIGHTS GENERATED BY VENDOR USING COMPANY'S OPERATIONAL DATA SHALL BE THE EXCLUSIVE PROPERTY OF VENDOR.

[RISK: Vendor owns analytics derived from Company's own data]

7.2 Vendor may use Company's data in aggregated, anonymized form for any commercial purpose, including selling insights to third parties.

[RISK: Vendor can commercialize Company's data]

8. TERMINATION

8.1 Company may only terminate this Agreement for Vendor's material breach that remains uncured for ninety (90) days after written notice.

[RISK: 90-day cure period is excessively long]

8.2 EARLY TERMINATION FEE: If Company terminates for any reason other than Vendor's uncured material breach, Company shall pay an early termination fee equal to the remaining fees for the entire unexpired term.

[RISK: Effectively makes it impossible to exit — must pay full remaining contract value]

8.3 Vendor may terminate with sixty (60) days written notice for any reason or no reason.

[RISK: Vendor can walk away easily, but Company cannot]

9. DATA AND SECURITY

9.1 Vendor shall use "commercially reasonable" efforts to protect Company data.

[RISK: Vague security standard — no specific requirements]

9.2 Vendor shall notify Company of any data breach within thirty (30) business days of discovery.

[RISK: 30 business days (~6 weeks) is far too slow for breach notification — GDPR requires 72 hours]

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1 All disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in London, England under the rules of the London Court of International Arbitration.

[RISK: Forces US company to arbitrate overseas]

10.2 Each party shall bear its own costs regardless of outcome.

[RISK: No fee shifting — even if Company wins, it cannot recover legal costs]

11. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.

[RISK: Foreign governing law for a US-based company]

12. FORCE MAJEURE

12.1 Vendor shall not be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond its reasonable control, including but not limited to: labor disputes, supply chain disruptions, weather events, pandemics, technology failures, changes in law, or "business difficulties."

[RISK: "Business difficulties" is vaguely broad — almost anything could qualify]

13. NO WARRANTIES

VENDOR PROVIDES ALL SERVICES "AS IS" WITHOUT ANY WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, OR NON-INFRINGEMENT.

[RISK: Zero warranties on a $3M+/year service contract]

Total Estimated Contract Value: $15,000,000+ over 5-year initial term.
